Our Mission

We are the Mwalimu James Japeth Foundation, and we are dedicated to uplifting the status of communities through various development initiatives. We do this by improving food security, health care, access to quality education, and peaceful coexistence between different groups.

Our Vision

Mwalimu james Japheth mwamu foundation Ltd envision a society where communities have the innovative, participatory and needs-driven approaches in an effort to realize meaningful development that enables them to excercsie their right to education and social security
