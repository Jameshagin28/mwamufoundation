About Mwalimu James Japheth Mwamu Foundation

#KISUMU MANYIEN

Our mission is to empower students to create their own futures, and we are driven by the belief that every person should have the opportunity to study and work in an environment free from discrimination. We believe that education is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to creating an inclusive learning environment for all. We seek to promote community building through both our programs and in various ways throughout our organization.
